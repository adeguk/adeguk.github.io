A Little bit about myself

My Story: So Far!

Being born into a family that has always encouraged utilizing to the fullest degree our natural gifts and abilities, I have excelled in every ventures and lifestyle I have undertaken. I have adopted my parents' standards of how to conduct oneself in life, disciplined, determined in one's pursuits and above all to work hard at everything you do. I have an inquisitive nature enjoy challenges and mysteries. I dedicate my life to the pursuit of happiness while resolved to take on the intellectual challenges. Now, more than at any other time, I had become obsessed to being an industry-wide must have leader with the ability to evaluate topic related to business and political operations.
Am Prince Adewale Ayodele Oluwaseun Adegoroye aka "L.O.Gessy", "Bobo-Logarithms", "Logg" and "Omo'ba". My family originated from Akure kingdom, Ondo State, Nigeria but born in Enfield, United Kingdom in April 1981. I had spent my teenage life in Nigeria where I had my elementary education to secondary school level. I graduated from University of Greenwich UK with BEng. Computer Systems and Software Engineering and MSc Business Information Systems, University of Bedfordshire UK.
By my living, working and effectively interacting with people of many lands, utilizing a cross-cultural and international insight coupled with the ability to speak two languages at a professionally proficient level, I have a thorough understanding of what it means to appreciate and respect cultural differences. This aid me tremendously as I work with diverse professional.
For the past ten years, my academic and professional careers have maintained a conceptual continuity, focusing on the Information and Communication Technologies. I have worked within multi-disciplinary teams; and anticipate utilizing these experiences to develop the world around me. People find me to be an upbeat, self-motivated team player with excellent communication skills. I spent several years working as both graphic and web designer, leading up design teams for independent consultancies; then decided to take my infatuation of the web world to the next level and formed Sitechum.
