My Story: So Far!

Being born into a family that has always cheered utilizing to the fullest our natural abilities, I have excelled in every lifestyle I have ventured. I have adopted my parents' standards of how to conduct oneself in life, discipline and tenacious in pursuit. I have an inquisitive nature enjoy challenges and mysteries. I dedicate my life to the pursuit of happiness while resolved to take on the intellectual challenges. Now, more than ever, I had become obsessed to being an industry-wide must have leader with the ability to evaluate topic relating to business and political operations.

I'm Prince Adewale Ayodele Oluwaseun Adegoroye aka L.O.Gessy, Logarithms & Logg. My family originated from Akure kingdom, Ondo State, Nigeria but born in Enfield, UK in 1981. I spent my teenage life in Nigeria where I had my elementary education to secondary level. I graduated from University of Greenwich UK with BEng Computer Systems and Software Engineering and MSc Business Information Systems from University of Bedfordshire UK. By my living and working in a cross-cultural environment with int'l insight, I've come to appreciate and respect cultural diversities.

For the past 10years, my academic and professional careers have maintained a conceptual continuity, focusing on the Information Technologies. I have worked within multi-disciplinary teams; and anticipate utilizing these experiences to develop the world around me. People find me to be an upbeat, self-motivated team player with excellent communication skills. I spent several years working as both graphic and web designer, leading up design teams for independent consultancies; then decided to take my infatuation of the web world to the next level forming Loggcity & Sitechum.
