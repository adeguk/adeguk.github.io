My Story: So Far!

Being born into a family that has always cheered utilizing to the fullest our natural abilities, I have excelled in every lifestyle I had ventured. I have adopted my parents' standards of how to conduct oneself in life, discipline and tenacious in pursuit. I have an inquisitive nature enjoy challenges and thrillers. I dedicate my life to the pursuit of happiness while resolved to take on the intellectual challenges. Now, I had become obsessed to being an industry-wide must have leader with the ability to evaluate topic relating to business and political operations.

I'm Prince Adewale Ayodele Oluwaseun Adegoroye aka Logg, L.O.Gessy & Logarithms. My family originated from Akure monarchy, Ondo State, Nigeria but born in Enfield, UK in 1981. I spent my teen life in Nigeria where I had my elementary education. I graduated from Uni. of Greenwich UK with BEng Computer Systems & Software Engineering and MSc Business Information Systems from Uni. of Bedfordshire UK. By my living and working internationally, I've come to appreciate and respect cultural diversities.

My academic & professional lines have maintained a conceptual continuity, focusing on Information Technologies in the last 10years. I have worked within multi-disciplinary teams; utilizing these experiences to develop the world around me. People find me to be an upbeat, dynamic team player with good communication skills. I spent several years working as both graphic and web designer, leading up design teams for independent consultancies; then decided to take my passion of the web world to the next level forming Loggcity & Sitechum.
